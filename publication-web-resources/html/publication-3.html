Our world is a gallery, echoing the stories of countless civilizations. While some have withstood the test of time, others have faded into obscurity, leaving behind only fragments of their artistic legacy.The challenge of documenting and preservingevery unique art form before it vanishes has not been easy.

This project delves into the potential of using non-invasive and cutting-edge technologies such as LiDAR scanning and photogrammetry not only to digitally archive endangered art forms but also to breathe new life into them. It traces my journey from identifying a site with an existing art form of the previous era, that is at risk of disappearing and to explore innovative methods for its preservation and physical reconstruction.

By bridging the gap between ancient craftsmanship an modern technology, the project seeks to uncover new ways of

safeguarding cultural heritage for future generations.
